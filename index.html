About FHINJA

FHINJA
Train Your Ninja. Strike the Market.

FHINJA is a next-generation AI trading companion built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC). Users create and train their own ninja-style agents to detect volatility, spot opportunities, and deliver real-time recommendations. Combining strategic automation with GameFi-style progression, FHINJA transforms trading into an engaging journey — train, test, and evolve your ninja in the market dojo.

Tokenomics $FHINJ

BUILT FOR THE COMMUNITY

$FHINJ is the utility token that powers the entire FHINJA ecosystem on Binance Smart Chain (BSC). With a fixed supply of 10,000,000,000 tokens, $FHINJ fuels agent creation, skill upgrades, performance rewards, staking, and ecosystem growth. Every allocation is designed to support long-term sustainability and empower our community dojo.
